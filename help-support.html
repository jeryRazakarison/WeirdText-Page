1. Getting Started

Welcome to WeirdText! This guide will help you get the most out of our text transformation app.

What is WeirdText?

WeirdText is an iOS application that transforms your text into various creative formats. From simple text effects to complex transformations, you can make your messages stand out with unique visual styles.

2. How to Use WeirdText

Basic Usage

  1. Open the WeirdText app on your iOS device
  2. Type or paste your text in the input field
  3. Tap the "Random Effect" button to apply a random effect for free
  4. Or select a specific effect from the available options (requires purchase)
  5. Copy the result to use in other apps

3. In-App Purchases

WeirdText offers all effects as premium content that can be unlocked through in-app purchases:

Available Packages

  • Individual Effects: Purchase specific effects for $0.99 each
  • All Unlocked: Get access to all current and future effects for $4.99
  • Random Effect: Always free - tap the random button to try any effect

Restoring Purchases

If you've previously purchased effects but they're not showing as unlocked:

  1. Go to the "Other" tab in the app
  2. Tap on "Restore Purchases"
  3. Wait for the restoration process to complete
  4. Your purchased effects should now be available
  5. Note: The Random Effect button remains free even if you haven't purchased any effects

4. Privacy & Security

WeirdText is designed with your privacy in mind:

  • Local Processing: All text transformations happen on your device
  • No Data Collection: We don't collect or store your text
  • Secure Payments: All purchases are handled by Apple's secure system
  • No Tracking: We don't track your usage or personal information

6. System Requirements

  • Device Compatibility: iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch
  • Storage: At least 20MB of free space
  • Internet: Required for in-app purchases

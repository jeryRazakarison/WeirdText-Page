1. Introduction

Welcome to WeirdText's privacy policy. This policy describes how we handle your privacy when you use our mobile application.

WeirdText is an iOS application developed by Nagord that allows you to transform text with different visual effects. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. WeirdText operates entirely on your device and does not transmit any information to our servers.

3. How We Handle Your Data

Since we do not collect any data, there is no data processing or storage involved in using WeirdText.

üîê Your Privacy is Protected

Important: All text processing happens locally on your device. We do not store, transmit, or access any of your text content or personal information.

4. Information Sharing

Since we do not collect any data, there is no information to share with third parties.

5. In-App Purchases and App Store

WeirdText offers in-app purchases to unlock additional effects. These transactions are handled by Apple's App Store:

  • We only receive basic information about your purchases to activate features
  • Your payment data is protected by Apple's privacy policies

6. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any data, there are no security measures needed for data protection.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there are no data rights to exercise. Your privacy is inherently protected.

8. Data Retention

We do not retain any data since we do not collect any information from our users.

9. Cookies and Similar Technologies

WeirdText does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies since we do not collect any data.
